FoundOPS was a web startup that provided field service logistics for small and mid-sized businesses. The company was founded by Jonathan Perl and Oren Shatken in February of 2011 in a spare fraternity bedroom at Purdue University. After a successful beta program and a first place finish in the 2012 Powder Keg competition, the team joined Angie's List, continuing their passion of making field service providers more efficient.

Thank you to all of the advisers, beta customers, and supporters who helped make FoundOPS a reality.
